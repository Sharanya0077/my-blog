About Me

Hi, I’m Sharanya.

I like writing about thoughts that sit quietly in the back of the mind — friendships, growing up, choices, and the in-between moments we don’t always talk about.

Alongside writing, I’m learning how to build things on the internet. This blog is both a creative space and a learning project — shaped slowly, intentionally, and with curiosity.

This isn’t meant to be loud or perfect. Just honest.
