The Door I Left Unopened

Jan 2025 · 5 min read

As a child, I wanted to be everything at once. An astronaut. A designer. A pastry chef. Each dream felt real, urgent, possible.

Somewhere along the way, I chose one door and quietly closed the others. This piece is about wondering — not regretting — who I might have been if I had chosen differently.

Maybe the doors we don’t open still shape us, just in quieter ways.
